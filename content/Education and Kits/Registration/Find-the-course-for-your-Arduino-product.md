Learn how to find the online course for your Arduino kit or other product.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **Activation is no longer required for most kits.** Many courses that previously required an activation code are now available for free. Open the course page to see if activation is required, or go to [About activation codes for Arduino products](https://support.arduino.cc/hc/en-us/articles/4402999992850-About-activation-codes-for-Arduino-products) to learn more.

---

## Find courses on Arduino Cloud

You can view and open courses from Arduino Cloud.

View courses:

1. Sign in to [app.arduino.cc](https://app.arduino.cc/).
1. In the sidebar, select **Courses**.

Open a course:

1. Put your cursor on the course to reveal the buttons.
1. Click the **Go to Course** button to open the course.

If you don't know which course to use, see [List of courses by product](#list-of-courses-by-product).
