---
title: "What do I need to participate in a webinar for CTC GO?"
id: 4409122660242
---

To participate in the live webinars for CTC GO!, you will need the following:

* **A compatible device:** A computer or mobile device that can run [Google Meet](https://meet.google.com/landing).
* **A stable internet connection:** To ensure a smooth experience, a reliable internet connection is recommended.
* **A webcam and microphone:** These are needed to interact with the instructor and other participants.

For the best experience, we recommend testing your setup before the webinar. See [Connect your video & audio
](https://support.google.com/meet/answer/10409699){.link-external} for help.
